About the role

Product managers at Zomato have a consistent track record of creating and growing great products for millions of users globally. You will be given maximum autonomy to solve interesting problems across many different areas of our business - working on high scale transactional products to social platforms to solving optimization problems on deep tech algorithmic systems. The potential to learn and create impact at the same time is immense. We are growing our team and are looking for motivated Product Managers to join us in our vision to provide better food for more people .

You need to be adept at first principles thinking, distilling insights from data and driving product roadmaps by rallying teams around the product vision. Clear communication, bias to action and great execution chops are a must have.

Here’s what you will do day-to-day

  • Fully own the vision and roadmap of the product areas you are assigned
  • Develop a deep understanding of our users, their needs and create useful product experiences that address those needs
  • Manage the cross-functional execution of the product roadmap, working closely with design, engineering, operations and business teams
  • Synthesize insights from user research, customer meetings, usage data and sales feedback to prioritize improvements and new feature ideas to achieve Zomato’s business goals
  • Lead organizational initiatives to establish best-in-class product management practices within Zomato

Who fits the bill?

  • Proven track record of managing all aspects of a product lifecycle and executing large cross-functional initiatives
  • B.E/B.Tech degree, or equivalent experience in a high growth tech company or startup. A strong technical understanding, from overall architecture to intricate details, is a major plus
  • Data-driven - solid understanding of data/analytics tools, and ability to deep dive into data to make decisions
  • User-centric approach and unwillingness to make compromises on the user experience
  • Ability to communicate seamlessly and clearly with all key stakeholders
