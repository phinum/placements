Description :

Summary of Role :

Work with the IP Development team on the circuit design, simulation, FE model development and verification of IPs in various technology nodes.

Essential Responsibilities :

Use industry standard design tools to perform gate/transistor level circuit design, verification, simulation with electrical, physical, FE and timing rules generation for IPs such as Standard Cell Logic libraries, IO libraries, and Efuse Macros & Memories.

Required Qualifications :

  • Requires a Bachelor, Masters OR PhD degree in Electronics
  • Be able to collaborate with program and technical design leads on multiple concurrent projects.
  • Should have excellent problem solving skills, written & oral communication, teaming & interpersonal skills
  • Perform all activities in a safe and responsible manner and support all Environmental, Health, Safety & Security requirements and programs

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Should have good understanding of VLSI Design and CMOS technology
  • Knowledge of the end-to-end IP design cycles
  • Knowledge in RF technologies (Bulk, CMOS & SOI) process is desired.
