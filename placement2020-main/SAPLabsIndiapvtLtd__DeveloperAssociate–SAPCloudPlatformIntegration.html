Description :

SAP started in 1972 as a team of five colleagues with a desire to do something new. Together,
they changed SAP, Originally known for leadership in enterprise resource planning (ERP)
software, SAP has evolved to become a market leader in end-to-end enterprise application
software, database, analytics, intelligent technologies, and experience management. A top
cloud company with 200 million users worldwide, SAP helps businesses of all sizes and in all
industries to operate profitably, adapt continuously, and achieve their purpose.
Do you want to be part of a great team who builds the next generation of successful SAP
software in the cloud? Join us, make your career as Developer and grow your skills while
working in an exciting environment. We are looking for talents with fresh ideas who want to
make a difference.
The product unit Cloud Platform Integration within SAP T&I Cloud Platform is providing cloud
integration technology to SAP applications, partners, and customers. We are responsible for
SAP Cloud Platform Integration, SAP’s strategic integration platform running on SAP Cloud
Platform. With more than 7000 customers, SAP Cloud Platform Integration is one of the most
successful cloud services at SAP. In line with SAP’s multi-cloud strategy our service is
running on various infrastructure providers including Amazon Web Services and Microsoft
Azure. PRODUCT TECHNOLOGY STACK
- Runtime: Modular Java runtime based on OSGi specifications.
- Scripting languages: Groovy, Python
- UI: JavaScript, CSS, HTML, SAP UI5, Eclipse
- Storage: RDBMS (Sybase ASE, Postgres), non-RDBMS (Mongo, Cassandra), InfluxDb time
series database
- Messaging: Apache ActiveMQ, Kafka
- Other frameworks: Apache CXF, Apache Camel, Hystrix and many other open source
frameworks
- Technical Connectivity Adapters: REST, HTTP, SOAP, LDAP, FTP, JMS etc.
- Business Connectivity Adapters: EDI, AS2, AS4, EB-XML etc.
- IaaS (Infrastructure as a service): SAP hosted cloud, AWS, GCP/GKE, Azure, OpenStack and
AliCloud
- PaaS (Platform as a service): Cloud Foundry
- CaaS (Container as a service): Docker/RKT, Kubernetes
Expectations
- Collaborate with a mentor from the vibrant architecture community.
- Demonstrate excellent quality, design, development and delivery skills in scrum teams
working in short development and release cycles.
- Use and extend Open Source frameworks heavily and possibly contribute to them as well.
- Contribute to DevOps for Cloud Integration
- Work with geographically distributed teams seamlessly
Education & Skill Requirements
- Strong analytical and problem solving skills
- Excellent understanding of data structures and algorithms
- Optional: Introduction to the concepts of functional and object-oriented paradigms
