About the company

Juniper Networks is the industry leader in network innovation. We stand as a response and challenge to the traditional approach to the network. We tackle all aspects of the network–across routing, switching and security–to help solve our customers’ toughest challenges so they can compete and thrive in the future. Our ground-breaking silicon, systems, and software transform the economics and experience of networking for service providers, enterprises, government agencies, and higher education organizations worldwide. Juniper enables high-performance networks that combine scale and performance with agility and efficiency, so customers can build the best networks for their businesses.

Job Description for Software Engineer 2 - Juniper Networks, Bangalore

Juniper is seeking exceptional engineering students to join hands in building the new network. As part of our engineering team, you will produce the world's largest and most demanding networks, including routing, switching and security solutions. As a potential expert in software, silicon, and systems, you will work closely with highly talented colleagues, to disruptively innovate and improve the New Network.

Technical Skills:

  • Extensive programming exposure in C, C++ or Python
  • Good knowledge in Multi thread, IPC and System programming
  • Good knowledge on Networking Fundamentals including TCP/IP, OSI layers.
  • Exposure to Cloud Technologies and Opensource projects including OpenStack, OpenShift, Kubernetes, etc.
  • The AI/ML knowledge would be an added advantage.

Soft Skills:

  • Analytical mind with an excellent problem-solving skills.
  • Excellent Written and Verbal Skills.
  • Very good team player with can-do attitude.
  • Ability to ramp-up fast on new and upcoming technologies.
  • Ability to work and collaborate with multiple teams.
