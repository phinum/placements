Software Developer will support a team of amazing people to work on developing solutions for product improvements and new features/functionalities. Requirements:

  • A passion for coding and an unquenchable thirst for learning.

  • Should ask tough questions and challenge the status quo
  • Solid grip on at least one programming language.
  • Good understanding of OOP concepts.
  • Basic knowledge of SQL and database design.
  • Prior experience in building any end-to-end application is a plus.
