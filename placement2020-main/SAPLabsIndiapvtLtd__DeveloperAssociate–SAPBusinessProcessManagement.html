Description :

SAP, Originally known for leadership in enterprise resource planning (ERP) software, SAP has
evolved to become a market leader in end-to-end enterprise application software, database,
analytics, intelligent technologies, and experience management. A top cloud company with 200
million users worldwide, SAP helps businesses of all sizes and in all industries to operate
profitably, adapt continuously, and achieve their purpose.
Our mission is to help our customers digitize their business processes. As the undisputed domain
expertsfor Business Process Management Technologies (incl. workflows, business rules, forms,
process visibility and process/rules mining) at SAP, we deliver products and services in the Cloud
as well as on premises.
We at BPM are a family of around 150 colleagues, distributed across 3 locations: Walldorf
(Germany), Bangalore (India) and Sofia (Bulgaria). We are proud of our unique team culture
and our engineering excellence. The teams build, deliver and operate the SAP CP Workflow
Management product consisting of the following Cloud Platform services: CP Workflow, CP
Business Rules, CP Process Visibility and Inbox.
The development focus for our services involves heavy evaluation and usage of cutting edge
technologies in the area of Cloud and Process Management (Kubernetes, Cloud Foundry,
Containers, JAVA, Spring, Java Script, RDBMS, NoSQL, Kafka, AWS, Azure, Devops etc). Our
development follows the principle of lean/agile and dev- ops where teams have end-end
responsibility of design, development and operation of services.
If architecting and developing large scale, distributed, resilient, scalable and performant cloud
services excites you, if working with cutting edge technology and open source products in the
field Workflows/Business Rules is something you are looking forward to you, we offer you the
opportunity to be part of the talented team and define this space.
Expectations
• Excellent programming know-how in Object Oriented Programming Language
• Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills
• Goal-oriented team worker with good communication skills and ability to work
independently, and eagerness to learn and explore new topics
Education & Skill Requirements
• Masters or bachelor’s Computer Science degree in Engineering with excellent academic
records
