About Jio Platforms Limited

Jio Platforms Limited (“Jio”), a majority-owned subsidiary of Reliance Industries Limited, has built a world-class all-IP data strong future proof network with latest 4G LTE technology (through its wholly owned subsidiary, Reliance Jio Infocomm Limited). It is the only network conceived and born as a Mobile Video Network from the ground up and supporting Voice over LTE technology. It is future ready and can be easily upgraded to support even more data, as technologies advance on to 5G, 6G and beyond. Jio will bring transformational changes in the Indian digital services space to enable the vision of Digital India for 1.3 billion Indians and propel India into global leadership in the digital economy. It has created an eco-system comprising of network, devices, applications and content, platforms, service experience and affordable tariffs for everyone to live the Jio Digital Life. As part of its customer offers, Jio has revolutionized the Indian telecom landscape by making voice calls for Jio customers absolutely free, across India, to any network, and always. Jio makes India the highest quality, most affordable data market in the world so that every Indian can do Datagiri.

About the Role:

We are looking for best-in-class UX/UI Designers who would help us build and design best in class designs and experience that we would all be proud of.  So we are looking for folks who are passionate learners, problem solvers, innovators and natural collaborators.

In essence, the buck stops with you!

Of course, you would have a bunch of awesome mentors and buddies to help you around…

We are team Jio. We believe that human-centered design, together with cutting-edge digital solutions can transform all aspects of human life. We are looking for talented & passionate UI & UX designers to join our team.

Responsibilities

  • Working in coordination with product managers and engineers to develop interactive designs for mobile Apps and website and continuously find ways to enhance designs and implementation.
  • Lead and evangelise best user-interface design practices, which create exceptional user experience.
  • Understand customer behaviour, psychology, business drivers and objectives, and translate them into efficient UI design
  • Have an understanding of UX design, user flow, prototypes and wire framing
  • Stay abreast with the latest UX design practices for mobile applications.
  • Understand material design concepts and have worked on them
  • Lead the design process by creating sketches, wire-frames, navigation flow charts, Photoshop mock-ups and interactive prototypes
  • Define design direction in close collaboration with other Designers, Researchers, Writer to define approach, tone, personality, ease of use, end-to-end flows.
  • Deliver detailed design flows and specifications, ensure product excellence throughout product development and implementation process.
  • Build strong relationships with cross functional stakeholders to drive user focus, creativity, efficiency, and product excellence.

Desired Skills

  • Excellent oral and written English communication skills
  • Knowledge of HTML, XHTML, JavaScript, Ajax, CSS, Flash
  • Familiarity with Responsive Web Design (RWD) techniques and tools is a plus
  • Ability to work both collaboratively as part of a team, and independently with minimal supervision, on multiple projects
  • Ability to turn around rigorous wireframes and mockups quickly in an iterative environment
  • Strong communication and presentation skills, ability to listen, be proactive at addressing feedback, represent and advocate for UX and users.
  • Great attention to detail

Requirements:

Strong experience with Adobe Creative Suite (Photoshop, Dreamweaver, Illustrator, XD,  InDesign, sketch etc.) or equivalent software

Desired Skills and Experience

Dreamweaver,Illustrator,Photoshop,Sketching,Wireframing
