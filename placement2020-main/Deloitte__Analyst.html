Nature Of Business : Consulting
Designation : Analyst
Tentative Job Location : Mumbai, Bangalore
Description :

Professionals in this Technology Consulting group offer both advisory and deployment services that provide clients with comprehensive solutions to help them better manage the critical business functions.

We accomplish this mission through continued development of people in our Technology Consulting Service Line – SA&MA: Analytics & Cognitive (A&C)

Delivery of specific capability in A&C:

 Understand the work assigned by project manager/Team leader

 Break down complex business problems and develop strategies to solve them

 Translate business objectives into analytic approaches and identify data sources to support

 Perform exploratory analysis on data, do data cleaning, data aggregation, build and test hypothesis, build analytical model, test and deploy model etc.

 Develop and apply statistical analysis methods such as regression , time series analysis, hypothesis testing and classification

 Understanding of statistical concepts like OLS, MLE, Probability, Sampling etc.

 Using machine learning techniques (clustering, decision tree, boosting and bagging models, deep learning etc.) and their real-world advantages/drawbacks.

 Understanding statistical computer languages (R, Python, SAS, and SPSS etc.) for modeling and draw analytical insights

 Ability to prepare presentation using insight from data and help in preparing story line to be able to present to audience in a layman language

 Adhere to defined processes and tools while performing day-to-day operations

 Supporting team members on working on solutions, client pitches and proposals

 Help in hiring, on-boarding and other office team activities
