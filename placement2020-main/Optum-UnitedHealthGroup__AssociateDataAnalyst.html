Position Title, Responsibility Level: Associate Data Analyst – Grade 25

Function: Enterprise Data and Analytics

Enterprise Data and Analytics (EDA) is an Analytics service delivery unit part of Optum Global Solutions. EDA provides data analytics, data science, business intelligence, data solutions and consulting services across various business segments within UnitedHealth Group enterprise. The team collaborates with business teams to identify analytics opportunities to bring down the total cost of care and improve quality of care to advance UnitedHealth Groups mission of “Helping People Live Healthier lives”

Primary Responsibilities

1. Understand business process and the underlying data for exploratory data analysis and insight generation to be consumed by the business leaders
2. Responsible to design and develop the analytics solution and deliver projects with appropriate analytics methodologies and techniques to solve business objectives
3. Part of end-to-end Data Science and Data Analytics projects lifecycle & acts as Horizontal capability for Data Science/ Analytics teams
4. Interact with business partners to understand the business problem and define the scope of the analytics solution
5. Create work estimate and plan the work deliverables as per the project plan
6. Peer review and follow project methodologies and guidelines

Preferred Qualifications

1. Degree in Engineering
2. Strong written and oral communication skills
3. Excellent problem-solving abilities
4. Strong interpersonal and team skills
