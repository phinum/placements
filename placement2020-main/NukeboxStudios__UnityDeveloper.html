Nature Of Business : Game Development
Designation : Unity Developer

Description :

If you’re a specialized Unity Game developement who can turn a game idea into code in a fast-moving environment, and if you like getting your heads and hands involved in various aspects of the game’s creation from concept to finished product including coding, programming, audio, design, production, and visual arts, we’d love to hear from you.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Provide technical leadership to single individuals and complete teams.
  • Participate in strategic planning & drive progression and innovation within our app development processes.
  • Support, guide, and motivate our engineers & review their performances.
  • Assist our producers and team leads.
  • Keep a close eye on the trend shifts and innovations early.
  • Involve in research and coding responsibilities.
  • Be with ready hands-on code whenever required.
  • Maintain a clean up-to-date work record to submit to the reporting manager.
  • Involve in regular discussion with Technical Architect to maintain technical production flow.

What we are looking for?

Must Haves:

  • Proven working experience in full lifecycle game development.
  • At least 1 years of game programming experience.
  • Exposure on Unity 3d.
  • Server-Side programming knowledge ( JS / Python / PHP / Perl ).
  • Knowledge of programming languages such as C++, C#, or Java.
  • Knowledge in designing multiplayer game models.
  • Extensive knowledge of production processes in game development.
  • A decent level of knowledge on Game Art, Audio, and Testing process.
  • Proven leadership abilities, strong communication skills.
  • Good capacity for teamwork and a strong ability to resolve problems and conflicts.
  • Good knowledge of project management methodologies and engineering practices.
  • Good command on English language (written and spoken).
  • Self-contained, motivated, and aim-oriented way of working & high level of self-motivation.

Must Haves:

  • Research Documents etc
  • Personal Blogs / Website
