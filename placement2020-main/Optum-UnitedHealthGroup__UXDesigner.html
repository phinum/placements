Company : Optum - United Health Group
Company Name : Optum - United Health Group
Nature Of Business : Optum Global Solutions
Designation : UX Designer
Tentative Job Location : Hyderabad/ Noida/ Gurugram
Description :

We are looking for passionate, opinionated and creative UI Designer who is responsible for designing
interactive responsive web based, mobile, and/or desktop applications, using Industry best practices and
standards, to deliver high value and quality.
Job Responsibilities
 Interacting with stakeholders to understand Business Requirements (verbal and/or written) and
effectively translate and apply them to create persuasive User Experience Design concepts for the
applications/ products
 Work with all kinds of passionate developers, BA&#39;s, and challenging clients to design and build new
features
 Investigating user needs and requirements, and identifying areas where constructive changes can
have maximum impact
 Building world class healthcare mobile application for iOS, Android and Hybrid platforms
 User interaction design for complex UI intensive applications: analysis of workflow, creation of
Lo/Hi-Fi mockups and prototypes, design consultations
 Closely work with developers to get the pixel perfect design and ensure that there is no error while
translating the wireframes &amp; visual design to code
 Conduct design workshops and presentations to transfer UX know-how to those interested within
UHG
 Innovate/prototype new ideas and expert in applying design thinking
Skill Requirements
 Fresh talent in UX Design with an awesome portfolio
 A degree/ post-graduation in design OR equivalent education from a reputed institute
 Expert in designing mobile apps for iOS, Android platforms following their respective design
guidelines
 Effective with popular design tools like: Sketch, Photoshop, Axure, and InVision
 Skilled in and well read on design thinking practices like user research, focus group feedback,
lightweight usability testing, rapid prototyping, etc.
 Excellent skills with quick design reviews and recommendations, and able to quickly make concept
sketches during stakeholder meetings
 Solid understanding of user-centered design principles
 Proven skills to create information architecture, navigation maps, wireframes and Lo/Hi-Fi
prototypes along with design specifications are a must
 Ability to drive projects from start to finish with minimal supervision and to juggle multiple projects
and priorities in a fast-paced, multi-disciplinary environment
 A good sense of composition, typography and visual balance

 HTML5 and CSS3 skills, with cross-browser, cross-platform compatibility; familiarity with basic
scripting, functional prototyping; translate visual design from mockups to front end templates will
be awesome to have, though not a must
 A good communicator with excellent negotiating and presentation skills
