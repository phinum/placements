Description :

Role and responsibilities

Business Analysts are core to our business model. Working as specialist members of small teams, they are responsible for:

  • Working directly with clients and FJ team members on a day to day basis to identify business needs, frame a solution, seek the data to solve the problem and then present recommendations to drive change
  • Building and maintaining client relationship by following high professional standards
  • Identifying information sources, understanding database structures and data dictionaries to develop the optimal approach for data extraction, validation, and combination
  • Developing predictive models under the supervision of a senior modeler
  • Conducting other quantitative and qualitative analyses. This will include reviewing publications and interviewing appropriate industry experts
  • Formulating definitive data driven recommendations and presenting them in a client ready presentation
  • Data extraction, preparation, validation, and manipulation using statistical analysis tools such as
  • SAS, R, Python and SQL may be required

In addition to these responsibilities, we encourage you to contribute in myriad ways limited only by your imagination.

What does Fischer Jordan offer?

Direct client interaction: This is not a back-office role. You will be interacting with clients from day 1 and will be offered opportunities to directly present your work during client meetings. Fischer Jordan management will continuously help you refine your presentation skills – verbal and written.

High growth environment: How fast you grow into leadership positions is only dependent on your performance. Semi-annual performance management and promotion cycles enable fast track to leadership responsibility.

Senior Level Mentorship (FJ): Opportunity to get training and mentorship directly from the partners at Fischer Jordan. Being a small team, each team member is encouraged to present their insights to the Partners on a regular basis.

Mentorship by industry leaders: A leading industry mentor (existing client/ past client) is assigned to each team member based on his/her preference and the mentor’s availability and willingness.

engage@fischerjordan.com

Full ownership of your workstreams: You are trained to work as an owner of all steps of your workstream. This includes problem identification, solution design, analysis, recommendations, and presentation

Unparalleled quality of work: Solve difficult and important problems for CXOs from Fortune 500 companies across multiple industries and domains. If you like the overlap of business, mathematics, and technology, and enjoy working in a fast-paced environment, this role is tailormade for you.

Travel to the US : Opportunity to work from the client site (in the United States) as and when required. It gives you a platform to learn by working side by side with clients and build professional connections.

Ecosystem of highly talented people: We hire from top engineering colleges (IITs and BITS) and Delhi University colleges and are committed to building a diverse team, thus ensuring holistic development of each team member.

Minimum Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s degree in any field and graduating in 2021
  • Minimum CGPA: 7.5. We will not accept applications with lower CGPAs
  • Possess excellent analytical skills and attention to detail. Hunger to keep up with the latest trends in advanced analytics
  • Hunger to learn advanced Excel, SAS, R, Python and SQL to extract, validate and combine data from multiple sources
  • Hunger to develop skills in building analytical models. This could range from Excel based models to more advanced predictive models
  • Strong communication skills enabling you to clearly communicate your thoughts/work to the client or internal teams both verbally and in writing
  • High levels of motivation, professionalism, and integrity
  • Strong commitment to teamwork
  • Willingness to travel to client meetings and do client-site work as needed
  • Ability to handle multiple workstreams and be able to deliver best results under high pressure situations
  • Entrepreneurial spirit
