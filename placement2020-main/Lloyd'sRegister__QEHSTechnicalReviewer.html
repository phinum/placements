Description :

Job Overview: -

Are you curious by nature and looking to develop your career to become an expert in Management System, working across a range of industry sectors? Are you looking for an independent technical role with a lot of freedom? Would you like to be part of an international organization and develop yourself further? Would you like to contribute to a safer world by sharing your knowledge and experience? If so, then Lloyd´s Register may be the right choice for you.

Job responsibilities: -

  • As a permanent technical reviewer at Lloyd’s Register, you will evaluate whether the recommendation for certification made by the lead auditor is adequate as per the international accreditation requirements and authorize certification to deserving clients of Lloyd’s Register.
  • With the numerous numbers of unique and interesting clients we have, no day for you will be the same. Your inputs as a technical reviewer the overall management system at the client’s place. The broadening and deepening of collaboration with your stakeholders is also a great aspect of your job.
  • Based on your expertise, you will contribute to the development of our services. This is a challenging and diverse job where you get to interact a lot of different people and have the opportunity to deal with very interesting (technical) challenges!
  • You will have the advantage to operate from where you are, therefore, you can expect a great work-life balance.

We are looking for: -

  • First class Bachelor’s degree in Engineering or Science from a reputed institute.
  • Around 2 to 3 years of industry work experience.
  • Good verbal, written and presentation skill in English language.
  • Ability to learn and apply the learning.
  • Critical thinking and decision making.
  • Innovative thinking, curiosity and creative thinking.
  • Results oriented and initiative taker.
  • Attention to details.
  • Proactive attitude and social skills.
  • Computer literate.

What does Lloyd’s Register offer?

  • Interesting job in a very innovative, diversified and international work environment working with client organisations ranging from small family owned firms up to global acting companies.
  • The reputation and success story of a company that has been active in the market for more than 250 years.
  • Opportunities for Continuous Professional Development.
  • Opportunity to qualify as lead auditor.

About Lloyd´s Register

  • The Lloyd’s Register Group (LR) is an independent risk management organization that helps to improve our clients’ quality, safety, environmental and business performance throughout the world. This is mirrored in our mission statement “working together for a safer world”.
  • LR is a global engineering, technical and business services organization and a maritime classification society, wholly owned by the Lloyd’s Register Foundation, a UK charity dedicated to research and education in science and engineering for the benefit of the community at large.
  • Our expertise and activities cover Marine, Energy (Oil, Gas, Chemicals and Power), Management Systems and other asset-intensive industries.
  • This role is specific for LR Management Systems.
