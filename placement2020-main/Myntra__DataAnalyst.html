Description :

Data analysts are a part of Business Insights, category planning, customer experience, revenue  operations, marketing and supply chain teams focused on specific functions or initiatives. They  work closely with various business functions to enable data driven problem solving and decision  making.

In a large ecommerce company such as Myntra where direct interaction with customers and  associates is limited, data is often the only source of truth and therefore plays a key role in  performance measurement and decision making. Data analysts work on three broad types of  problems to enable this -

Descriptive - problems involving performance measurement, setting up meaningful metrics,  granularity, visualisation and benchmarking for various business functions Investigative - design of experiments, determining success of experiments or initiatives;  hypothesis testing, deep dive analysis around specific events or observable trend changes Predictive or Prescriptive - deeper level of problem solving using statistical, machine learning or  operations research techniques to generate estimates, forecasts or recommendations for better  planning or goal achievement within business functions

Data analysts should have a flair for structured problem solving and are typically skilled on SQL,  Python, R and other applications that handle big data. They should also possess good applied  knowledge of commonly used statistical concepts, machine learning and operations research  techniques.
